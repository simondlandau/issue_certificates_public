License for Insurance Brokers and Insurance Consultants

It is possible to act as an Insurance Agent without a License

There are three different permits which are distinguished from each other:

  • Permit as an insurance representative. As an insurance representative you are contractually bound to one or more insurance companies during your brokerages and therefore you represent the interests of these insurance companies.
  • Permit as an insurance broker. As an insurance broker, you broker insurance policies without being contractually bound to one or more insurance companies. You should represent the interests of insurance customers in a neutral manner when selecting suitable insurance products for them.
  • Permit as an insurance advisor. As an insurance advisor, you do not broker insurance. Therefore, you do not receive brokerage commission from the insurance companies and are also independent of these. You develop concepts for the correct customer-specific insurance coverage and the suitable insurance companies in this respect. You only work in the interests of the customers and are only paid by them.
  • To obtain a permit, the following requirements typically apply:
    • reliability
    • ordered financial circumstances
    • a corresponding qualification
    • a professional indemnity insurance policy
  • The permit must be applied for prior to starting business activities.
  • Our team assists applicants and existing intermediaries with documentation, renewals, and any compliance updates.

Details

Prerequisites
  • You possess the necessary reliability for the operation of a commercial business.
    As a rule, persons who have been subject to criminal sanctions imposed by a court for one of the following infringements in the last five years are not considered to be reliable:
    • Criminal offence
    • Theft
    • Embezzlement
    • Extortion
    • Fraud
    • Breach of trust
    • Money laundering
    • Falsification of documents
    • Receiving stolen goods
    • Profiteering
    • Insolvency offences
  • Your financial circumstances are sound.
    You do not fulfil this requirement if:
    • insolvency proceedings are opened against your assets or
    • have been rejected due to lack of assets or
    • you are entered in the register of debtors.
  • The taking out of professional indemnity insurance
  • Vocational qualifications and relevant professional experience.
Procedure
  • You can complete your application for licensing by registering online below.
  • A decision will be made on the application as soon as all completed documents have been submitted.
  • The license is granted for an indefinite period.
    • It will expire only when the businessman no longer requires the license.
    • You may not start work until you have received the permit.
Required Documents
  • Completed application form.
  • Relevant Identity documents.
  • Copy of your articles of association.
  • Proof of orderly financial circumstances.
    • When checking personal reliability, in individual cases we may request additional documents, appropriate to your personal reliability as an applicant.
Costs
  • Charges are incurred to grant the license, the amounts differ and you will be invoiced once your documents have been reviewed and your licence approved.
  • The current cost is US$100 per successful licence application.
  • Additional costs may be incurred if we request additional documentation during the procedure.
Processing Time
  • Two to ten days, depending on the documentation required.
